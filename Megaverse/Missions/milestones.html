Section One: Data Management

Cultural algorithms are processed at client terminal. Initial packages are hosted on main servers supported at local county administration offices. At regional offices cultural data is processed and stored appropriately. Algorithm development data is obtained from information contained in textbooks, personal experience of development teams, self-led field interviews and from respective radio and television programs, which send journalists to probe cultures. Intercounty data-links to come in handy.
