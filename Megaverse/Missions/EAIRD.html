East African International Recruitment Drive

Recruitment to be carried out of students in public universities, technical training institutes, secondary schools and vocational training facilities all around East Africa for a start. Sponsored certificate courses to help incorporate talented artisans. Recruits to receive appropriate training and essential support towards meeting objectives in respective home areas where recruits are expected to have deep understanding and essential networks. Students to fill-in reports through specialized forms contained in digitized log-books. This mission calls for review of the log-books currently used by students for industrial attachment for purposes of adjustment to suitability.
