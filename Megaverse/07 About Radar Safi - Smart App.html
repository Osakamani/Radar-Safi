Mission Objective

The main objective of Radar Safi is to match geographically tagged skillsets to geographically tagged individual and social needs. The system seeks to align and channel cummulative human endeavor into positive work for homes and environments. Radar Safi engine is provisioned with logical and strategic handles for nesting with applications from various sectors of life.
